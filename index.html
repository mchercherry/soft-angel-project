SAP's Conception

SAP (Soft Angel Project) was officially founded in 1941, but the discovery of cannies can be traced back to 1919. An unidentifiable creature was found miles away from a military base, with a nest spread across a 10 mile radius. While probing the suspected nest yielded no identification nor further signs of life, this first creature (affectionately called an angel by an on-sight private) revealed a world wide phenomena several countries were beginning to endure. Dubbed "cannies" for their human-like qualities, our program seeks to solve the Great Canny Mystery.

Cannies In America

SAP™ has now officially had 4 encounters with American-found cannies. It is not clear how these creatures connect to one another, if at all, as they all have been located in varying locations and states of mind; some are more human-like than others, some have even been able to function as "human" in our society prior to being discovered. Despite these differences, each creature mimics human-esc qualities, from their body structures to their communication abilities. Several researchers have brought on their own theories for these evolutionary traits.
